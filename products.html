    How to Use

    • Take 6-8 Cordyceps fruit bodies in 200-250 ml water.
    • Keep boiling Cordyceps bodies in water for 1 minute.
    • Now, put the flame at the lowest setting, cover the vessel with a lid and let it simmer for another 14-15 minutes.
    • Serve and drink your refreshing Cordyceps tea preferably after the meals.
    • Take Cordyceps tea twice a day for full effects.

    Consult a physician in case of pregnancy or any other medical condition. Not recommended for people with autoimmune diseases or below 5 years of age.
